ABOUT THE EMERALD


Welcome to The Emerald, San Francisco's freshest music hot spot where the music is as diverse as the city itself! Nestled in the heart of this vibrant musical landscape, The Emerald is your go-to place for an unpretentious, good time. Indie rock, funk, folk, or any other genre you can think of, we've got something for everyone. With killer line-ups every night, including some of the hottest up-and-coming bands around, you're in for a memorable musical experience.

We've got more than just great music. Our talented bar team are here to craft delicious cocktails that'll have you grooving to the beat all night long. And when the late-night munchies hit, our tasty snacks are the perfect accompaniment to keep the good times rolling. Our laid-back environment feels like your living room, but cooler. It's the ideal place to unwind, let loose, and enjoy the music with friends old and new. The Emerald is your home away from home in the heart of the city.




FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS


Age Restriction: Is there an age restriction to enter The Emerald?

  • Yes, all guests must be 21 years or older to enter The Emerald.

Getting Here: How do I get to The Emerald?

  • You can easily reach us by public transportation; The Emerald is conveniently located near several bus stops.
  • If you prefer to drive, there's ample parking available nearby, and we're just a short ride from major highways.

Outside Food and Drink: Can I bring outside food or drinks into the venue?

  • To ensure the best experience for all our guests, outside food and drinks are not allowed at The Emerald. Don't worry; we have a fantastic menu of snacks and cocktails ready to satisfy your cravings.

Booking Events: How can I book an event at The Emerald?

  • Booking an event at The Emerald is easy! Just reach out to our team through our booking page, and we'll work with you to plan and host your special occasion in our unique and welcoming space.
